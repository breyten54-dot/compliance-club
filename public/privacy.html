Privacy Policy

Praeto Compliance Club, operated by Berkeley Pretorius (FSP 1457), South Africa · Last updated: 13 July 2026

This policy explains how the Praeto Compliance Club ("we", "the platform") collects, uses, and protects your personal information, in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Information we collect

  • Account details: your name, email address, password (stored only as a secure hash), FSP licence number, and phone number.
  • Membership & payment records: your membership tier, subscription status, and payment records. Card details are handled entirely by our payment provider (PayFast) and are never stored by us.
  • Usage data: CPD records you log, compliance alerts you have read, and your conversations with the AI Compliance Advisor.

How we use it

  • To provide the member portal, CPD tracker, compliance alerts, template library, and AI Compliance Advisor.
  • To manage your membership and process subscription payments.
  • To send you service communications and compliance alerts you have opted into.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing.

Processing partners

We use trusted operators to run the service on our behalf: PayFast (payment processing), and cloud hosting and database providers. AI Compliance Advisor conversations are processed by Anthropic to generate responses. These operators process data under their own security and privacy commitments; all data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

Your rights under POPIA

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may object to processing. Email berkeley@praeto.co.za and we will action your request. Deleting your account removes your personal details from active systems; records we are required to retain for legal or accounting purposes may be kept in line with those obligations.

Contact

Information queries: berkeley@praeto.co.za
